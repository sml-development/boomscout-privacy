Boomscout Privacy Policy

Boomscout does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information — or any information at all. The app runs entirely offline and makes no network connections of any kind. Your game statistics, daily streak, and any in-progress board are saved only in your Roku device's local storage (the device registry); they never leave your device and are deleted by the system when the app is removed. Boomscout contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, no accounts, and no third-party services.

Questions? Contact lush.home@icloud.com.
